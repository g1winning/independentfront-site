Australia's only sustainable comparative advantage is its mineral and agricultural endowment. The previous model — raw ore shipped offshore, finished goods reimported — captured a small share of the total value. The new model captures the processing margin domestically, on cheap, clean nuclear power, moved by electrified rail. The infrastructure that makes that possible is the largest line item in the Sovereign Wealth Fund.
