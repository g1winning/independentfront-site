Talk to us.

The Front is available, openly, to consult with any independent candidate who wants to adopt these policies. The form below routes directly to outreach@independentfront.com. Tell us who you are and what you'd like to discuss; the more context we have, the more useful the response.

Anything you want the Front to know — the seat you're considering, the position you'd like to test, the section you'd like clarified, the journalist deadline, the volunteer offer. The more context, the better the response.

Submissions route via FormSubmit to outreach@independentfront.com. The Front does not store form data outside that email inbox. Spam-detected submissions are blocked at the FormSubmit layer.

What happens next

Submissions are reviewed by the Front. Candidate and organisation enquiries receive a tailored response within five business days. Press enquiries are responded to within 24 hours where the timeline permits. General feedback is logged and read but does not always receive an individual reply.

If your enquiry concerns a specific seat, electorate, or political situation, indicate it in the message field. If your enquiry is time-bound to an election, indicate the date in the timeline field above.

For volunteer offers, expect a follow-up that asks for more detail on availability, expertise, and the kind of work you'd like to do. The Front operates without paid staff; volunteer contributions are how the platform iterates.
